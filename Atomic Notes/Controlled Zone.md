# Controlled Zone

A subnet that protects the internal network from the [[Uncontrolled Zone|uncontrolled zone]].

There are several types of networks within the control zone.
- [[Demilitarized Zone]] (DMZ)
- [[Internal Network]]
- [[Restricted Zone]]
